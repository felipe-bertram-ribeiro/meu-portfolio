About Me

A bit of my journey, learnings, and passion for technology.

Who I am

My name is Felipe Bertram, I am a developer and a Systems Analysis and Development student, currently in the 4th semester. I have always enjoyed understanding how things work and transforming ideas into real solutions through programming.

My journey

Since I started studying technology, I have been diving into languages like C#, Python, and SQL, while also exploring areas such as automation, web development, and geoprocessing with QGIS. I enjoy working on projects that involve real challenges and constant learning.

Focus and goals

My main goal is to keep evolving as a developer and expand my knowledge in Software Engineering. I am interested in creating efficient, intuitive systems that make a real difference in people's and companies’ daily lives.

More than code

I believe technology is made of people. Each project I develop represents part of my story, dedication, and willingness to learn. I always strive to deliver the best — with organization, aesthetics, and purpose.
